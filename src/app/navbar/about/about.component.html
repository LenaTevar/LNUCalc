What do you need or want to fly solo

Posted on August 22, 2019

If for whatever the reason you cannot follow the courses programmed from LNU - Computer Science, you don't need to give up. If you fullfill some requirements, you can still get a diploma in Computer Science (but without the fancy title).

The requirements in credits

  • 90 credits in Computer Science DV -> Datavetenskap
    • At least 2 courses in advance level 2dv###. The final project is included in this section.
  • 90 credits free standing
    • 30 credits must NOT be from computer science NO #dv###
    • 60 credits in whatever you want, including computer science.

The requirements were given by the person in charge of the programs in that moment. I always recommend to talk with the responsible person at that moment and/or the student counselor before taking any action. This web is just orientative and not official.
